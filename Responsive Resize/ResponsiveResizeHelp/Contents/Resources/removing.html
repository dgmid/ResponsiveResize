Removing Devices

You can easily remove Devices from the Devices bar from the Devices Panel.

  1. Open the 'Devices Panel' by selecting 'Add / Remove Devices…' from the Devices menu or from the Customize menu , or use the keyboard shortcut ⌘D.
  2. Hover over the Device you want to remove in the Devices list.
  3. Click on the icon that appears on the device name.
  4. The Device will be instantly removed from the Devices bar.
