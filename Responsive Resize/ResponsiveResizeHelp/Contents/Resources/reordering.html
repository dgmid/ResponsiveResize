Re-Ordering Devices

You can easily reorder the Device buttons in the Devices bar from the Devices Panel.

  1. Open the 'Devices Panel' by selecting 'Add / Remove Devices…' from the Devices menu or from the Customize menu , or use the keyboard shortcut ⌘D.
  2. Click and drag the device you want to move until it is in the position you want.
  3. The buttons in the Devices bar will be automatically updated.
