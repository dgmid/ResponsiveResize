Adding Preset Devices

You can add devices to the Devices bar form a preset list.

  1. Open the 'Devices Panel' by selecting 'Add / Remove Devices…' from the Devices menu or from the Customize menu , or use the keyboard shortcut ⌘D.
  2. Select a device from the drop-down menu in the 'Select Preset Device:' box.
  3. Click 'Add Device' to add the device to the Devices bar.

NOTE: As well as adding preset devices you can define your own devices and add then using the 'Create New Device' options.
